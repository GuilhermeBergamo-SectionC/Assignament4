The game Awards

Welcome to The Game Awards website, where we celebrate the best in video games and unveil what's next in gaming. This year promises an exciting lineup of new games, showcasing innovation and creativity from around the globe. Join us for a night where we honor past achievements and reveal future experiences that will captivate gamers everywhere. Be part of the journey that continues to shape the vibrant world of gaming, all here at The Game Awards.

Games that will be displayed

Persona 3 Reload

The game follows the protagonist, who balances their daily life of going to school and building relationships with others with fighting the monstrous Shadows during the Dark Hour.

The wicther 3

The monster slayer Geralt of Rivia must find his adoptive daughter who is being pursued by the Wild Hunt, and prevent the White Frost from bringing about the end of the world.

Metaphor Rephantazio

The game takes place in a medieval European-inspired fantasy setting that is considered the "mirror" to the contemporary backdrop of the real world, and follows a non-silent protagonist who must embark on a journey to protect their kingdom.

Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is an open-ended country-life RPG! You've inherited your grandfather's old farm plot in Stardew Valley. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life. Can you learn to live off the land and turn these overgrown fields into a thriving home?
